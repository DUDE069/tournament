Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

NPC's Esports Tournaments ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and age.
  • Gaming Information: In-game usernames, player IDs, and team details.
  • Financial Information: Payment history, withdrawal details (e.g., UPI ID), and billing addresses. Note: We do not store complete credit card numbers; these are securely processed by our payment gateway (e.g., Razorpay).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To organize tournaments, verify participant identities, and distribute winnings.
  • To process payments and facilitate withdrawals.
  • To communicate with you regarding tournament updates, platform changes, or customer support.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment gateways to process transactions securely.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and financial data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Contact Us Regarding Privacy

If you have questions about your data, please contact us at support@npcesports.com.
