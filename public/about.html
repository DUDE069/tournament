About Us & Our Services

What We Do

NPC's Esports Tournaments is a premier competitive gaming platform dedicated to hosting high-quality, skill-based mobile gaming tournaments. We provide a seamless, secure, and professional environment for teams and individual players to compete against each other for prestige and cash prize pools.

Our Services

  • Organized Tournaments: Daily and weekly scheduled matches with varying skill tiers.
  • Fair Play Enforcement: Strict moderation and verification processes to ensure a level playing field without cheaters.
  • Secure Prize Distribution: Integrated payment systems (such as Razorpay) to seamlessly collect entry fees and quickly distribute winnings directly to the winners' accounts.
  • Live Leaderboards: Real-time tracking of team performances, kills, and overall rankings across seasons.

Pricing Structure

Our pricing is completely transparent. The entry fee for each specific tournament is clearly displayed on the tournament card before you register. Entry fees vary depending on the total prize pool and the scale of the event (typically ranging from ₹50 to ₹500 per team).

There are no hidden subscription fees. You only pay when you choose to register for a specific event.

Our Mission

We aim to empower the grassroots esports community by providing players with a robust platform where they can monetize their gaming skills securely and competitively.
