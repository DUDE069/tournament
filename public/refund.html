Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

To ensure fair play and proper prize pool management, NPC's Esports Tournaments has a strict policy regarding refunds and tournament cancellations.

1. Entry Fee Refunds

General Rule: All entry fees paid for tournament registrations are final and non-refundable, except under the specific circumstances listed below.

Once a user or team registers and the payment is verified, the slot is locked, and no refunds will be issued if the user decides not to participate or fails to show up on time.

2. Exceptions (When Refunds Are Issued)

We will initiate a 100% refund of the entry fee under the following conditions:

  • Tournament Cancellation: If NPC's Esports Tournaments cancels a tournament for any reason (e.g., insufficient participants, technical failures).
  • Technical Errors on Our End: If a server outage on our platform prevents the tournament from taking place.
  • Payment Failures: If your money is deducted but the slot is not allotted due to a gateway error.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment method (Credit/Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking) used during registration.

Please allow 5 to 7 business days for the refunded amount to reflect in your bank account, depending on your bank or payment provider.

4. How to Request a Review

If you believe you were charged in error, please contact our support team at support@npcesports.com or +91 9876543210 within 24 hours of the transaction, providing your transaction ID and team details.
